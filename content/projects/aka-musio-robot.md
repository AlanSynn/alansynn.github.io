Musio is a social robot designed by AKA Intelligence, powered by **MUSE** - a deep-learning-based AI engine for communication developed by AKA. Using deep-learning algorithms, Musio converses with people, recognizes objects, and understands people's facial expressions.

For two years I was an engineer at AKA Intelligence, working on running various applications on embedded devices and tackling the problem across the system stack:

- Developed and optimized **MusioOS** (Android-based) and device drivers.
- Implemented a circuit-defect detection system.
- Stabilized power consumption across the AP with sleep control.
- Designed in-house devices for flashing.
- Implemented in-house software for the production stage.
